# Getting Started

## Hobby Land

`Hobby Land` is a place to keep track of your hobbies. Add hobbies that are going well, 
just started, or need further pushes.

When you add a hobby to the `Hobby Land`, you provide a rank or weitage. The higher the
rank, the better you are doing with the hobby. A lower ranked hobbies need attention. `Hobby Land` 
reminds you that you need to get better at your hobbies.
